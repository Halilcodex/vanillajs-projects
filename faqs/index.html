Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
What are FAQs

The letters (or word) FAQ stands for Frequently Asked Questions. A FAQ page is usually a list of common questions people have asked about a specific product or service. Every business and product website comes with a set of default pages like an “About” page, “Contact” page, “Privacy Policy” page, and more. A FAQ page is one of the few must-have pages on this list.
